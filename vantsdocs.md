# Vants Mini Assignment #

## Explanation ##

The first alteration that I did was I separated the vants into three groups and made the direction they move different based on that. One group goes left, one group goes right, and one group continues straight. When I was messing around with this at first, it created some really interesting patterns. I got a lot of diagonal lines and I realized it was because I was having an initial turn that was then edited. I decided on changing it to have some vants not turn at all. The second alteration I did was having the vants that did not turn lay down extra pheromones. This created a larger trail and a high chance that the other vants worked off of them more.  Lastly, I tried to color each section of vants to help better visualize the three different behaviors. I struggled a little bit to make sure that things were tracking properly, but I think it ended up working out in a really cool way.
